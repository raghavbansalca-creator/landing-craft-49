Iterations on the Selected Direction

8 explorations keeping the single-stroke "7" + serif wordmark as the constant

Selected Direction — Base

Single-Stroke + Serif Wordmark

Minimal outline "7" as a gradient line stroke (indigo → blue), Playfair Display wordmark, "VISION" in small tracked caps. Refined and modern without being cold. The 8 iterations below vary colour, layout, weight, and accent elements while keeping this DNA intact.

Selected Direction
Iteration A
Warm Gold — Amber to Crimson
Same stroke, warmer palette. Feels more human and approachable — less tech-startup, more trusted advisory.

Iteration B
Dark Mode — Violet to Sky
Lighter gradient tones on dark navy. Strong for pitch decks, presentations, and dark-themed digital surfaces.

Heavy Stroke — More Assertive
Same gradient path at 2× stroke weight. More visual presence — lands between "minimal" and "bold." Good for large-format use.

Iteration F
Emerald to Teal — Growth Palette
Fresh green → teal gradient. Signals growth and feels less "finance-typical" — good for sustainability or startup-adjacent positioning.

Iteration G
Monochrome Navy — Print Ready
Single colour, no gradient. Necessary for letterpress, embossing, fax, and any context where gradients can't reproduce cleanly.
